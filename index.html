Follow the Journey of the Waddle Duck!

About the Project

Waddle Island Adventures is an indie game being developed by me, a solo developer passionate about creating fun and immersive experiences. You play as a brave green duck who must save the world from an evil frog by gliding through lush forests, treacherous swamps, and mysterious caves.

This dev log will document my progress, challenges, and milestones as I bring the game slowly to life. Stay tuned for regular updates every month!

Dev Log Updates

Update #4: Flappy Waddle Prototype!

Date: January 15, 2025

During the break, I started a side project called Flappy Waddle — a game inspired by Flappy Bird, but with way more content. You’ll guide Waddle the Duck through obstacles, collect coins, and unlock new backgrounds, pipe styles, and skins. It’s quick, fun, and a great creative outlet while I plan the next big steps for Waddle Island Adventures.

Update #3: Taking a Short Break

Date: December 5, 2024

Hey everyone! Just a quick note — I’m taking a short break from Waddle Island Adventures to clear my head and recharge. Solo development can be a grind, and I want to make sure the project continues to be something I enjoy working on. I’ll be back to it soon, better than ever!
