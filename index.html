Join the Journey of The Flopa Life — A Skateboarding Life Sim!

About the Project

The Flopa Life is a chill skateboarding life sim where you rebuild a skate park, craft custom boards, and explore a cozy town full of characters. As a skater, you’ll manage your energy, collect resources, and unlock cool gear while living the laid-back skater lifestyle.

This dev log will document my solo journey as a developer creating this world, sharing progress, challenges, and sneak peeks. Expect fresh updates every few weeks!
