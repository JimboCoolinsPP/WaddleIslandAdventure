About the Project

Waddle Island Adventures is an indie game being developed by a small team passionate about creating fun and immersive experiences. You play as a brave green duck who must save the world from an evil frog by gliding through lush forests, treacherous swamps, and mysterious caves.

This dev log will document our progress, challenges, and milestones as we bring this game to life. Stay tuned for regular updates!
