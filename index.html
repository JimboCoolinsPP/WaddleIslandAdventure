About the Game

In Waddle Island Adventures, you play as a brave little green duck on a mission to save the world from the clutches of an evil frog! Armed with your wits, courage, and the ability to glide through the air, you'll explore lush forests, treacherous swamps, and mysterious caves to uncover the frog's sinister plans.

With your unique gliding ability, you'll soar over obstacles, navigate dangerous terrain, and outsmart the frog's minions. Can you stop the evil frog and restore peace to the island?

Features

  • Play as a heroic green duck with the ability to glide
  • Explore a vibrant and dangerous world filled with secrets
  • Battle the evil frog and his minions
  • Solve environmental puzzles and unlock new areas
  • Use gliding to navigate tricky terrain and reach hidden locations
  • Stunning visuals and an immersive soundtrack
